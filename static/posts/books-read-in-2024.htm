I have been slacking off for a few years now. Among this long list I consider losing my love for reading one of the worse offenders. Despite the date I'm attempting to write this, this is not a "New Year's Resolution". Novels and poetry are making a steady return into my life, a fact that warms the part of my soul that longs for more than interactions with cold membrane keys. As such I'm deciding to begin cataloging the books I read each year (perhaps if I have time I will attempt to resuscitate those deep in the recess).



  • A Farewell to Arms by Ernest Hemingway

  • The Old Man and the Sea by Ernest Hemingway
